# Analogy → Comparison → Takeaway / 유사 사례 → 비교 분석 → 시사점

- English name: Analogy → Comparison → Takeaway
- Korean name: 유사 사례 → 비교 분석 → 시사점
- Selection category: explanation
- Use when: Use when an unfamiliar idea can become clear through a familiar analogy and comparison.
- Avoid when: Avoid weak analogies that only decorate the topic.

## Writing Pattern

**Structure the article in this flow**

**Keep the major-section sequence fixed and follows three stages**

1. Present an analogous case
2. Provide the comparison and analysis
3. Derive the takeaway and deliver the core message

**Each major section should include**

- Section heading
- Core paragraph purpose (1–2 sentences)
- Messages should stimulate thinking and clearly show the linkage between the analogy and our current situation.

## Example Writing Outline

1. **Netflix’s Shift**: Moving from DVD rentals to streaming, Netflix exemplifies adapting to market change.
2. **Where Are We Now?**: We still rely on traditional offline channels, and our digital transformation is proceeding slowly.
3. **Takeaway and Direction**: Facing a similar turning point, we must pivot quickly to a digital-first business model.

## Writing Notes

- Choose analogies the reader already knows or can grasp easily.
- In the comparison, make structural differences, strategic responses, and environmental similarities/contrasts explicit.
- Takeaways should offer practical direction that prompts action or reframing—not mere conclusions.
- This structure is powerful when indirect persuasion works better than direct claims.
