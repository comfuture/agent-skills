# Trend → Example → Action (TEA) / 트렌드 → 사례 → 실행

- English name: Trend → Example → Action (TEA)
- Korean name: 트렌드 → 사례 → 실행
- Selection category: trend
- Use when: Use when a trend should be made credible through examples and translated into action.
- Avoid when: Avoid when no timely examples are available.

## Writing Pattern

**Use a fixed three-part structure**

- **Trend**: The major direction or shift related to the topic
- **Example**: A concrete case where the trend is applied
- **Action**: An execution strategy that reflects the trend
- When presenting a trend, don’t just list facts—explain why it matters now.

**Each major section should include**

- Section heading
- Core paragraph purpose (1–2 sentences)

## Example Writing Outline

1. **Generative AI Is Reshaping Content**: Gen‑AI adoption in text, image, and video is rapidly mainstreaming and reshaping the market. (Trend)
2. **Case: Canva’s AI Auto‑Design**: With AI that auto‑generates designs from user prompts, Canva doubled MAUs. (Example)
3. **Our Response Strategy**: We’ll integrate Gen‑AI into core services to automate planning→creation→distribution. (Action)
4. **Expected Effect and Risk Control**: We expect shorter production time and higher satisfaction; a quality‑assurance program will uphold trust. (Action)

## Writing Notes

- A valid Trend reflects structural change and staying power—not a fad.
- For the Example, add numbers, comparisons, or performance to raise credibility.
- In Action, focus on feasible steps for the current organization/reader.
- Keep a clear link between Trend and Action to sustain the logic: “Therefore, we must do X.”
