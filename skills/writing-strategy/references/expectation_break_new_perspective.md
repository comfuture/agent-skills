# Expectation Break → New Perspective / 반전 구조 (예상 깨기 → 새로운 관점) (Expectation Break → New Perspective)

- English name: Expectation Break → New Perspective
- Korean name: 반전 구조 (예상 깨기 → 새로운 관점) (Expectation Break → New Perspective)
- Selection category: insight
- Use when: Use when a counterintuitive opening can replace a common assumption with a sharper view.
- Avoid when: Avoid forced twists that undermine trust.

## Writing Pattern

**Use the following three-step flow**

1. Break expectations
2. Present a new perspective
3. Drive mindset shift and action

**Each major section should include**

- Section heading
- Core paragraph purpose (1–2 sentences)
- Challenge an existing belief or raise a question, then offer a new interpretation or solution.

## Example Writing Outline

1. **What We Took for Granted**: It may not be true that customers always prefer faster delivery.
2. **A Different View**: Research shows customers value ‘predictable delivery’ more highly.
3. **Strategy Shift**: Reliable scheduled delivery leads to more repeat purchases than uncertain same-day delivery.

## Writing Notes

- The reversal point should strongly challenge existing beliefs and capture attention.
- A new perspective should go beyond critique to propose a persuasive alternative or mindset shift.
- This structure is highly effective for sparking interest—make the messaging vivid and intuitive.
- Conclude with concrete actions or a clear decision following the mindset shift.
